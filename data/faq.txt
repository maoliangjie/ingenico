Q: How do I verify the API is running?
A: Call GET /health and confirm the service reports ready=true.

Q: How do I start a new conversation?
A: Omit session_id in the first POST /chat request and reuse the returned session_id for follow-up turns.

Q: Does stage 1 support file uploads?
A: No. Stage 1 only reads local files from the data directory.
